Children's Museum of Santa Barbara

Responsive Design / Logo Design / Sitefinity CMS / SASS

The Children's Museum of Santa Barbara is set to open in June 2016, and the team needed a new website to promote their opening. Using a simple Sitefinity template, I created a fun and child-friendly look by heavily modifying the styles and implementing a new logo. The site is fully responsive and provides a mobile-friendly experience. I trained the Children's Museum team to utilize the content management system to update content wherever needed.
